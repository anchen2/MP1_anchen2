About Me

Abigail (Abbie) Chen

Graphic Design Major @ UIUC

Hi my name is Abbie Chen, I am a student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign studying Graphic Design and minoring in Computer Science. I will be graduating in the Spring of 2026.

Currently on campus I am a member of Design For America (DFA) a RSO that builds community projects through the Human Centered Design Process. Currently, I am a Co-Team lead for one of the Environmental Sustainability Team where we are developing a project that aims to reduce student waste. We have designed an app that gives students a space to buy and sell their old school supplies and unwanted clothing.

My design processes values audience research and branding to solve challenges and provides Human Centered solutions.
